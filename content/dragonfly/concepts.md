# Core Concepts

Understanding the fundamentals of Dragonfly's architecture.

## PXE Boot

Preboot Execution Environment (PXE) allows machines to boot from the network before any operating system is installed.

## Metal Management

Dragonfly tracks and manages your bare metal inventory, providing a unified view of your infrastructure.

## Provisioning Workflows

Define how machines should be provisioned based on their role, location, or other attributes.
